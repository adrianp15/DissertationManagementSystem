Features

Increase in student autonomy

As students are always informed, knowing what to do,how to do it and when it needs to be done, students are able to work independently without needing as much supervisor time.

Improvement in feedback quality and thesis quality

With the right tools in place, feedback becomes better and more relevant, leading to better thesis work from the student.

Decrease in supervisor time spent on administration and beginners' questions

With the structure in place, supervisors can use their hours on their students’ essays rather than on administrative work related to their students’ essays.

Increase in thesis completions

The dissertation management system can lead to a major increase in completed theses by making the dissertation writting process more efficient
